ABOUT

CAIMB Interiors is a boutique interior design firm based in the Montreal Area. Our team of designers specializes in blending our modern aesthetic with each client’s personal design style. We take on a wide range of projects, including new construction projects, full-home renovations, and furnishing and styling projects.

Get in touch

We are here to provide design consulting, furniture and styling, and full-service renovation and design services. We are located in the Montreal, QC, but are open to taking on remote projects as well. Please fill out the form with a brief description of what you’re looking for, and we will get back to you within 48 business hours. We look forward to hearing from you!
